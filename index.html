Help

General

This family tree viewer is web and mobile compatible. It works on most modern browsers (if you're using Internet Explorer, good luck).


To move, just use your mouse to drag the tree. To see more detailed information, just click on a person. However, some family members are hidden. This will be marked with an arrow, which will look something like this:

   

You can click on people with these arrows, and the hidden tree (parents or children) will expand

Menu

The menu can be found in the top-left (where the Help button is). The first two buttons ( and ) allow us to zoom in and out.


The Index button opens up a list of all the family members, sorted alphabetically by surname. To go to a family member, just click on their name


(Since you're reading this, I assume you know what the Help button does)


The search bar can be used to find family members. Just start typing, and results will drop down as soon as you have typed at least 3 letters.

Shortcuts

There are a couple shortcuts you can use to make life easier:

Zoom

Zoom in:  +

Zoom out:  -

Reset zoom:  0

Navigation

Up a generation:  ↑

Previous sibling:  ←

Next sibling:  →

Down a generation:  ↓

Switch to spouse:  Tab
